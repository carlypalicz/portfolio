Behind the Lens

Hi, I'm Carly! Nice to Meet You! :]

I'm a 24 year old photographer located in Hoboken, New Jersey with a passion for all things music. I've been going to gigs since early high school, and have more recently gotten directly involved in the industry after picking up concert photography, a lifelong dream of mine.

I cover shows of all sizes, and can be seen anywhere from house shows and DIY spaces to some of the most iconic venues in New York. I'm particularly passionate about supporting local music, and have worked closely with some friends in local bands while also covering more well known artists for the publication I work with, f28 Music Media Live.

I'm always looking for new folks to work with, and am available for local bookings and small tours. Whether it's live show coverage, behind the scenes, or portraits - I'd love to capture you!

Thanks for checking out my site! Please don't hesitate to contact me for questions, collaboration, or just to connect!
