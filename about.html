Behind the Lens

Hi, I'm Carly! Nice to Meet You! :]

I'm a 24 year old self taught photographer located in Hoboken, New Jersey with a passion for all things music. I've been going to gigs since early high school, and have more recently gotten directly involved in the industry after picking up photography and shooting some local shows in NYC and New Jersey - plus one show in the middle of the desert, because I also believe in embracing spontaneity and love to travel when I can.

I'm particularly passionate about supporting local music, and as such have been attending/shooting a lot of smaller shows - ranging from stripped back acoustic sets in parks, to DIY house shows in Jersey backyards, to midwest emo moshpits in Brooklyn. I'm no stranger to larger crowds either, though, and am looking to shoot all kinds of gigs.

Now that I've gotten the hang of things on my own, I'm looking for people + publications to work with! I'd love to shoot more shows, and I'm open to other shoots and work as well, such as music write ups, features, or even interviews.

Thanks for checking out my site! Please don't hesitate to contact me for questions, collaboration, or just to chat!
